Lin Dan


Lin Dan also known as "Super Dan" is a chinese badminton singles player who is a G.O.A.T. He was born on 14th October 1983 in Fujian,China.

He has won many international tounments, the most significant of them are

  • Olympic gold medalist Bejing(2008) and London(2012)
  • Five time World Champion
  • Six time All England Champion
  • 66 BWF titles

Career

He made his debut in Asia Junior Championship in 2000 and emerged as winner in both team event and singles category .

After that his winning strike continued. His career win/loss record is 666/128.

Lee Chong Wei, a Malasyian badminton player and Lin Dan's rivalry is often considered as the greatest rivalry in the histroy of badminton. Lin won the head to head by 28-12.
